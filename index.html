about us

technology meets creativity

Aligning strategy with innovation, Codeit enables institutions and businesses to implement transformative digital solutions.

We are one of Saudi Arabia's leading digital advocates. Codeit's collaboration with the Ministry of Education will benefit the next generation of leaders on a national scale.

Through maintaining a diverse and internationalised portfolio as well as operating additional subsidiaries in the medical supply, automotive and real estate sectors, our parent company, Awab Holdings, is at the forefront of change in Saudi Arabia. We leverage Awab's extensive network and expertise to further our mission, widening the scope of digital transformation across industries.

Digitisation is a fundamental requirement of progress — not the goal. In prioritising the ultimate objective (higher-achieving students, enhanced workflow or improved customer interaction), not just the process, we are unique.

Outstanding managerial systems necessitate bespoke solutions implemented and cared for by a team of dedicated specialists.

origin story

In searching for an ERP to optimise the internal processes of Awab Holdings, Founder Fawaz Alotaibi was struck by the inadequacy of existing platforms and realised there was a knowledge gap in the Saudi Arabian IT market.

Alotaibi assembled a team of industry experts who developed a system to evaluate and answer client IT needs and began offering external ERP consultation services. Soon after, a government contract to work on ITIL protocol was secured — a critical juncture initiating Codeit's involvement in data warehousing, IT security and SLA agreements. Eventually, Alotaibi and his team were supervising end-to-end IT projects for multiple matrix organisations. Codeit was born.

Codeit's roster of clients includes some of the country's most influential businesses as well as its most vital institutions: the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Municipalities and Rural Affairs and the Ministry of Labor and Social Development.

industries

Codeit's unprecedented work in the education sector allows schools to impart the requisite aptitudes for success when it comes to digital awareness and the responsible use of technology.

We enable industry leaders in the private sector scope to prioritise the critical deployment of new technology. Increasingly, companies must control costs while ensuring data integrity. Our work in the public sector enables government agencies to identify areas where additional or updated IT infrastructure can resolve shortcomings. Public institutions often rely on antiquated systems; modernisation cuts costs while improving end-user satisfaction.

preview:

Digital responsibility and fluency are imperatives when it comes to developing Saudi Arabia's next generation of professionals.

Codeit's education pilot project, in conjunction with Saudi Arabia's public school system, enables over 35,000 students across the country access to a platform whereby they can build these skills.

The innovative software, developed by ITWORX, allows students to interact responsibly in a transparent network with their peers and teachers, encouraging them to excel, keep to deadlines and solve problems with critical thinking — not just by rote knowledge.

The student's development, rather than the technology, is the goal; the connections they build in this formative community promote positive digital behaviours to help them navigate their continuing academic careers and professional futures.

By 2020, Codeit's education project will grow to encompass two million students. Through this expansion, Codeit is set to become one of the world's largest providers of education software platforms.
